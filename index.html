Shopping List

Add - You can add any item you want. Just type the item name and click "ADD" button or press "Enter" key.

Toggle - When you have purchased the required item, you can strike-through each items. Just enter its corresponding number and click "Toggle" button or press "T-alphabet" key.

Remove - If u want to remove any particular item in the list. Just enter its corresponding number and click "Remove" button or press "Delete" key.
